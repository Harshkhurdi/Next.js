Lasagna Recipes

Sure, here’s a classic lasagna recipe:
Ingredients:
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (14 ounces each) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 3 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (optional)
Instructions:
  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the skillet; sauté until onions are softened.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta or cottage cheese with 2 cups of mozzarella and 1/2 cup Parmesan.
  6. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture. Repeat layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan on the top layer. Optionally, garnish with chopped parsley.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  9. Allow it to cool for a few minutes before serving.
Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
